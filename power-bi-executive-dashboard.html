Project Overview

This dashboard was built to help leadership move away from static spreadsheets and scattered weekly reports. It combined key business metrics into a single Power BI experience with drilldowns, filters, trend analysis, and executive summary pages.

The goal was to make performance easy to scan at a high level while still allowing managers to investigate why a metric changed.

Business Problem

Stakeholders needed faster answers to recurring questions: which region is growing, which channel is underperforming, where the pipeline is leaking, and which customer segments are driving revenue. Manual updates were slowing weekly reviews and creating version-control issues.

Solution Built

  • Star-schema data model with clean relationships between sales, customers, calendar, region, and product tables.
  • DAX measures for revenue, conversion rate, average order value, variance, growth rate, and target achievement.
  • Executive overview page with headline KPIs, trend cards, and clear visual hierarchy.
  • Drilldown pages for sales funnel, region, customer segment, and product-level performance.
  • Interactive slicers and tooltip views to help non-technical users explore metrics without touching raw data.

Dashboard Experience

The dashboard was structured for quick executive scanning first, then deeper analysis. Stakeholders could start with headline KPIs, then move into funnel, geography, product, and customer views without needing separate reports.

  • Executive KPI layer for revenue, conversion, target achievement, and trend direction.
  • Drill-through pages for root-cause analysis across region, segment, channel, and product categories.
  • Clean filter strategy so business users could answer specific questions without breaking the view.
