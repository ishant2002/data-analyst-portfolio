Project Overview

This project replaced repetitive manual sales reporting with an automated process. The system gathered raw sales exports, cleaned them, calculated KPIs, generated performance summaries, and prepared data for dashboards or stakeholder updates.

The focus was speed and reliability: remove manual copy-paste work, reduce spreadsheet mistakes, and make weekly reporting easier to repeat.

Business Problem

Sales teams often spend hours preparing the same reports every week. Manual reporting creates delays, inconsistent formatting, calculation mistakes, and slow visibility into performance changes. Leadership needed a cleaner system that could refresh quickly and produce dependable outputs.

Solution Built

  • Automated data cleaning workflow for raw sales exports, product categories, regions, and sales owner data.
  • KPI calculations for revenue, target achievement, month-over-month change, top products, and sales variance.
  • Repeatable report generation structure for weekly sales summaries and performance highlights.
  • Looker Studio or spreadsheet-ready output tables for dashboard refreshes.
  • Exception checks for missing values, incorrect formats, and unusual revenue changes.

Automation Design

The workflow was built to make weekly reporting repeatable. Raw exports could be placed into a defined input structure, cleaned automatically, transformed into KPI tables, and refreshed into dashboard-ready outputs.

  • Automated cleaning for product names, sales owners, regions, date fields, and revenue values.
  • Variance logic for comparing current performance against previous period, target, and category benchmarks.
  • Exception checks for missing values, unusual revenue movements, and incomplete sales owner mapping.

Business Impact

The automation saved recurring reporting time, reduced manual errors, and made sales performance easier to monitor. Stakeholders received more consistent reports, while the sales team spent less time preparing data and more time acting on insights.
